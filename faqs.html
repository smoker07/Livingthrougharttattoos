Permanent Tattoos and Piercings (I would want to elaborate a bit)
◦ Keep your tattoo clean (clean it twice a day using wet wipes, non alcoholic or baby wipes are recommended)
◦ Moisturise your tattoo twice daily using antibiotic and unscented moisturisers (cetaphil body lotion is recommended)
◦ Avoid contact with water and soap for 10-12 days. Use a plastic sheet or baby oil to save from it.
◦ Don't pick on your tattoos, Peeling might cause damage to the skin and may cause cause fading
◦ Wear breathable clothing over your tattooed area, as friction due to synthetics material may cause irritation or redness.
◦ Always clean your hands before touching the tattooed area.
Keep your piercing clean using saline solution. Take a bowl of lukewarm water, add two pinches of salt in it and mix. Take a cotton bud and clean both side of your pierced holes properly. Do this twice a day for 15 days for best results.
You may have to follow this routine more in case of different piercing positions.
